Frequently Asked Questions

Is the mentoring service really free?

Yes, the basic mentoring service is completely free. While there are additional premium services, basic consultation and advice are provided at no cost.

What kind of mentors do you match?

We match you with experienced mentors who share your nationality, visa type, and academic background. For example, if you're a Chinese student, we'll connect you with a Chinese mentor who has D-2 visa experience and the same major.

Can I use the service even if I'm not fluent in Korean?

Of course! We provide multilingual support services, and you can receive consultation in English, Chinese, Japanese, and more. We also offer special programs for language learning.

How are jobs verified?

All jobs are reviewed by legal experts to confirm legality, and employer information is thoroughly verified. We also collect reviews from actual workers to ensure safety.

Can I use the service regardless of my visa status?

Yes, while available services may vary depending on visa status, mentoring services are available to all internationals. For job matching, we only provide legal opportunities that match your visa status.
